Last updated: 2026

Ogi Lingo (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Ogi Lingo mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

- User ID (UID)
- Email address
- Display name
- Profile picture

### Gameplay Data
- Scores
- Missions
- Inventory
- Daily rewards

### Advertising Data
- Advertising ID (AAID)
- Device info
- Ad interactions

## 5. Account Deletion

You can delete your account in-app:

Settings → Hesabımı Sil

This will permanently delete:
- Account
- Scores
- Inventory
- Username
